Air pollution has been shown to have HARMFUL HEALTH AND PRODUCTIVITY EFFECTS.

Air pollution health impacts

Short term: itchy eyes, nose and throuat, wheezing, coughing, shortness of breath, chest pain, headaches, nauseau, bronchitis and pneumonia, asthma.

 

Long term: lung cancer, cardiovascular disease, chronic respiratory illness, developing allergies, heart attacks, strokes.

 

Click [here] to learn more.

Air pollution also affects productivity

The [OEDC] estimates that 1.2 billion working days are lost every year because of air pollution, and that this number will be at 3.7 billion by 2060.
